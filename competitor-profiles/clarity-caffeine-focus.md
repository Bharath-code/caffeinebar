## Positioning & Messaging

**Primary value proposition**: "The world's first app to visualise exactly how caffeine peaks and tapers inside your body" — helps users time caffeine deliberately around deep focus, physical training, and quality sleep. "Performance isn't about more caffeine. It's about better timing."

**Target audience**: High-performers, biohackers, athletes, and knowledge workers who rely on caffeine for productivity but want to optimize timing and minimize sleep disruption.

**Positioning angle**: Performance optimization tool, not a health tracker. Positions caffeine as a performance tool to be strategically deployed. Uses scientific language (pharmacokinetic curve) to establish credibility. Differentiates from "counting" apps by focusing on timing and strategy.

**Key messaging themes**:
- "Performance isn't about more caffeine. It's about better timing."
- Pharmacokinetic curve visualization — "see how caffeine peaks and tapers"
- Strategy-driven: "build a caffeine strategy that works with your biology"
- Three target zones: deep focus, physical training, quality sleep
- "Most people caffeinate by feel. Clarity changes that."
